## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

- Ruby 3.0+
- Rails 6.0+
- Basic understanding of Rails controllers
- BetterController installed (`gem 'better_controller'`)

Remember: BetterController is **Bring Your Own Services/Serializers**. The guides show patterns for integrating your chosen service and serializer libraries.
